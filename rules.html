Rules and Guidelines

General Rules

  • Participants must be students of Sri Krishna Arts and Science College.
  • Participants must register your teams with the Registration link available in the website.
  • Each participant can only be part of one team.
  • All teams must mail the PPT to ctds2025@gmail.com on or before Febuary 03, 2025.

Submission Guidelines

  • All submissions must be in PDF or PPT format.
  • Submit your ideas before the deadline.
  • Include team details and contact information in your submission.

Judging Criteria

  • Originality and creativity of the idea.
  • Feasibility and practicality of implementation.
  • Clarity and quality of the presentation.
